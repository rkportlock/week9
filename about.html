About us:

JP's was born out of a passion for Surfing and the Ocean in general and wanting to share both with the rest of the world. What was once a small side job for a couple of surfers, has turned into a fulltime job with multiple locations. With dozens of instructors with years of experience, you are in good hands at JP's.

There are few things in life that are as enjoyable as the feeling you get the first time you pop up on a surfboard. Here at JP's we believe in trying to get everyone to experience that feeling in as little as one lesson. All equipment is included with the cost of a lesson, so need to worry about finding the right board or wetsuit, we will have all of that ready for you, you just need to show up and be ready to have fun.
